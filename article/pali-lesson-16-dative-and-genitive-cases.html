Stem (词干) Dative singular (与格单数)

vidū viduno, vidussa (to or for the wise man)

Exercises

(a) Translate into English

1.Aggi uṭṭhāya kuṭumbikassa gehaṃ ḍahi.
Fire rose up and burnt the householder's house.
火燃起来了并且烧掉了屋主的房子。

Aggi:fire 火
uṭṭhāya: having risen 已生起
kuṭumbikassa:? family
gehaṃ: a house 一个房子
ḍahi: burnt 烧

2.Mayaṃ ajja isino assamaṃ pabbataṃ abhiruhissāma.
We will today climb up the mountain to see the seer's hermitage.
我们今天将会爬上山去看先知的隐居处。

Mayaṃ: we 我们
ajja: today 今天
isino: seer, sage 先知, 智者
assamaṃ: monastery, hermitage 寺院，修道院
pabbataṃ: mountain; hill 山；山丘，小山

3.Navo setu kārunā kato hoti.
The new bridge has been built by the carpenter.
新的桥已经被木匠建起来了。

Navo: new, fresh 新的，新鲜的
setu: bridge 桥
kārunā: ?
kato: make, built 做，修建
hoti: is, becomes 是，成为

4.Gahapatino gāvo corehi hatā.
The householder's oxen were taken away by thieves.
屋主的牛被贼牵走了。

Gahapatino: householder 屋主
gāvo: cow, cattle 牛
corehi: thief, robber 贼，强盗
hatā: ?

5.Gahapatino ucchavo dhanikena vāṇijena kītā.
The bouscbojder's sugar cane were bought by the rich merchant.
屋主的甘蔗被富有的商人带走了。

Gahapatino: householder 屋主
ucchavo: sugar cane 甘蔗
dhanikena: wealthy man 富有的人
vāṇijena: trader, dealer 交易商，贸易商
kītā: bought, purchased 买，购买

6. Sabbaññunā desito dhammo vihāraṃ gatehi senānino puttehi suto.
The Dhamma expounded by the Omniscient One was heard (listened to) by the general's sons who had gone to monastery.
一切知者所宣说的法，被到寺院的将军的儿子们所听到。

Sabbaññunā: omniscient; all knowing; with complete understanding 一切知者
desito: preached (by); taught (by); explained (by); instructed (by) 被教导;被解释
dhammo: nature, character 自性，法
vihāraṃ: 寺院
gatehi: gone (to), went (for) 去到
senānino: general 将军
puttehi: son, child 儿子，孩子
suto: heard, learned 听到，学到

7.Alagaddena daṭṭho migo tatth'eva patitva mato.
The deer bitten by the snake fell down and died there itself.
The deer bitten by the snake fell down and died there itself.
被蛇咬伤的鹿倒地而死。

Alagaddena: water-snake; poisonous snake 水蛇；毒蛇
daṭṭho: bitten 被咬
migo: deer 鹿
tatth'eva: right there; in that place; on the spot; there and then 就在那
patitvā: having fallen down (from); having fallen off (of); having fallen (onto) 倒下
mato: dead 死

8.Sūdehi gahapatino sevakānaṃ odano pacito.
The rice was cooked by the cooks for the householder's servants.
米饭是由厨师为户主的仆人煮的。

Sūdehi: cook; chef 厨师
gahapatino: householder; landowner 屋主；户主
sevakānaṃ: servant, attendant 仆人，侍者
odano: rice; boiled rice; food 米饭
pacito: cooked 煮的

9.Suriyassa ālokena andhakāro apagato.
The darkness was dispelled (or was gone off) by the sun's light.
黑暗被阳光驱散（或消失）。

Suriyassa: sun; lit. shining 太阳
ālokena: light; brightness 光
andhakāro: darkness; blackness; blindness 黑暗
apagato: without; free (from); with no 驱散

10.Bhūpassa ca kumārānaṃ ca amaccānaṃ ca bahavo abhinavā pāsādā kārūhi katā.
Many new palaces have been built by the carpenters for the King, the princes and ministers.
许多新宫殿已经被木匠为国王、王子和大臣建立起来。

Bhūpassa:
ca: and, both 和
kumārānaṃ: prince 王子
amaccānaṃ: minister 部长；大臣
bahavo: many; much; lots (of) 许多
abhinavā: brand new; fresh 新的
pāsādā: palace 宫殿
kārūhi: did, made 做
katā: done; did; completed 完成

Exercise b 英译巴

1.The ox beaten with a bamboo by the householder's servant ran away.
牛被户主的仆人用竹子打后逃跑了
Go gahapatino sevakena veḷunā pahaṭo apadhāvi.

Go: cow; cattle 牛
gahapatino: householder; landowner 屋主；户主
sevakena: servant, attendant 仆人；侍者
veḷunā: bamboo 竹子
pahaṭo: hit; struck 打；击打
apadhāvi: ?

2.The asetic climbed down (from) the mountain and entered the village for alms.
苦行者从山上下来，进村乞食。
Tāpaso (isi) pabbatamhā orūḷho gāmam pindāya paviṭṭho.

Tāpaso: ascetic; hermit 苦行者，隐士
isi: seer; sage 哲人；智者
pabbatamhā: mountain; hill 山；山丘
orūḷho: descended, climbed down 下来
gāmam: ?
pindāya: ?
paviṭṭho: entered; gone (into) 进入

3.The man having seen the thief that entered the house, went and brought the policeman
那人看到小偷进了屋子，就去把警察带来了。
Puriso gehaṃ paviṭṭhaṃ coraṃ disvā gantvā rājapurise ānesi.

Puriso: man; person 男子；人
gehaṃ: house; dwelling 房子；居住
paviṭṭhaṃ: entered; gone (into) 进入
coraṃ: thief; robber 小偷; 盗贼
disvā: having seen; having understood; having found out 看到
gantvā: having gone (to); having travelled (to) 去到
rājapurise: king’s man; royal servant; police officer 警察
ānesi: brought; took 被带走

4.The householder saw (his) son, fallen from the tree and died, and wept.
户主看见儿子从树上掉下来死了，哭了。
Gahapati rukkhato (rukkhamhā) patitaṃ mataṃ puttaṃ disvā rodi.

Gahapati: householder; landowner 屋主；户主
rukkhato: tree 树
rukkhamhā: tree 树
patitaṃ: fallen (onto); landed (on) 掉落（在...上）
mataṃ: dead 死
puttaṃ: son; child 儿子；孩子
disvā: having seen; having understood; having found 看到
rodi:cried, lamented 哭泣

5.The house bought by the mercant was burnt by his enemies.
商人买的房子被敌人烧毁了。
Vāṇijena kīto geho arīhi daḍḍho.

Vāṇijena: trader; dealer 交易商；贸易商
kīto: bought; purchased; paid for 买了的
geho: house; dwelling 房子；住宅
arīhi: enemy 敌人
daḍḍho: burnt; burnt down; consumed by fire 烧，烧掉，被火摧毁

6.Men go to heaven by means of Dhamma.
人们通过佛法进入天界。
Manussā dhammena saggaṃ

Manussā: human being; man; person 人类，人，人们
dhammena: 佛法
saggaṃ: heaven; paradise 天界；天国

7.Even the heroes were attacked by death.
就连英雄也遭到了死亡的袭击。
Vīrāpi maccunā pahaṭā.

Vīrāpi: ?
maccunā: death 死亡
pahaṭā: hit; struck 打，撞击；打击

8.Alas, the world will perish!
唉，世界就要灭亡了！
Nassati vata loko!

Nassati: perishes; ends; is destroyed; 灭亡；毁灭
vata: oh!; oh no!; oh dear!; wow! （感叹词）唉
loko: world; plane of existence 世界

9.Rice cooked by the cook was eaten by the beggar's dog.
厨师煮的饭被乞丐的狗吃掉了。
Sūdena pacito odano yācakassa sunakhena khādito.

Sūdena: cook; chef 厨师
pacito: cooked 煮的
odano: rice; boiled rice; food; 米饭；煮熟的米饭
yācakassa: beggar; mendicant 乞丐
sunakhena: dog 狗
khādito: eaten; consumed 吃掉

10.The man's oxen were lost (vanihshed).
该男子的牛丢失了（消失）。
Purisassa goṇo naṭṭho.

Purisassa: man; person 男子，人
goṇo: ox; bull 牛，公牛
naṭṭho: lost; perished; destroyed 丢失，丧失
