lokuttara-kusalacittāni - 4

出世间善心 4种

1.Sotāpatti-maggacittaṃ.
1.Path consciousness of stream-entry.
1.入流道心

2.Sakadāgāmi-maggacittaṃ.
2.Path consciousness of once-returning.
2.一来道心

3.Anāgāmi-maggacittaṃ.
3. Path consciousness of non-returning.
3.不还道心

4.Arahatta-maggacittañ cā ti.
4.Path consciousness of Arahantship.
4.阿拉汉道心

Imāni cattāri pi lokuttara-kusalacittāni nāma.
These are the four types of supramundane wholesome consciousness.

dosamūlacittāni - 2

Consciousness rooted in hatred 2
嗔根心 2种

1. Domanassasahagataṃ paṭighasampayuttaṃ asankhārikam ekaṃ.

  1. One consciousness, accompanied by displeasure, associated with aversion, unprompted.
    一心，伴随着不悦，与厌恶有关联，主动的。

2. Domanassasahagataṃ paṭighasampayuttaṃ sasankhārikam ekan ti.
One consciousness, accompanied by displeasure, associated with aversion, prompted.
一心，伴随着不悦，与厌恶有关联，被鼓励的。

mohamūlacittāni - 2

Consciousness rooted in delusion 2
痴根心 2种

1.Upekkhāsahagataṃ vicikicchāsampayuttam ekaṃ.

  1. One consciousness, accompanied by equanimity, associated with doubt.
    一心，伴随着镇静，与疑有关联。

2.Upekkhāsahagataṃ uddhaccasampayuttam ekan ti.
2.One consciousness, accompanied by equanimity, associated with restlessness.
一心，伴随着镇静，与掉举有关联。
