Pali Sandhi rules 巴利连音规则

Created At：2023-12-23 09:46 Updated At：2024-01-02 21:01
Keywords: pali

Pali Sandhi refers to the phonological rules in the Pali language, which is an ancient Indian language closely related to Sanskrit. Sandhi rules govern the changes that occur when two words come together in a sentence. These changes can involve the merging, deletion, or modification of sounds at the word boundaries. Pali Sandhi is important for understanding the correct pronunciation and interpretation of Pali texts. It is a topic of study in the field of Pali linguistics and is often taught in courses on Pali language and literature.
