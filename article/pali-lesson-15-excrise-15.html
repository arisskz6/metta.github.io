Lesson 15 Excrise 第15课 练习

巴译英：

Exercise a:
1.Ahaṃ hiyyo gāmamhā idhāgato.
I came here yesterday from the village.
我昨天从村庄来到这里。

2.Puriso rukkhamhā patitvā mataṃ puttaṃ disvā rodi.
The man wept after seeing the son that fell from the tree and died.
在看到儿子从树上摔下死亡之后，那个男子悲伤哭泣。

3.Senāpatinā arihi muñcitaṃ bhātikaṃ disvā gahapati ativa tuṭṭho ahosi.
The householder saw the brother saved from enemies by the general and became extremely happy.
屋主看到弟弟被将军从敌人手中解救出来，非常高兴。

  1. Suriyo uggato hoti, tumhe pana idāni pi sayatha.
    The sun has risen, but you are still lying down (lit. even now).
    太阳已经升起，但你还躺着（现在也是）。

  2. Therena anusiṭṭā manussā pāṇātipātā viratā ahesuṃ.
    The men advised by the Thera abstained from killing.
    这些人在长老的建议下放弃杀生。

  3. Te dhammaṃ caritvā saggaṃ gatā.
    They practised the Dhamma and went to heaven.
    他们修习佛法，去到了天界。

  4. Kuhiṃ ṭhito tvaṃ gehaṃ coraṃ passi?
    Where did you stand and see the thief that came to the house?
    你站在哪里看到贼去屋子里的？

  5. Patto hathamhā patiso bhinno ahosi.
    The bowl fell from the hand and was broken.
    钵从手上掉下来摔破了。

  6. Senāninā asinā pahaṭa arayo patitā matā.
    The enemies attacked by the general with (his) sword fell down dead.
    被将军攻击的敌人带着（他的）剑倒地身亡。

  7. Buddho bhikkhāhi ca upāsakehi ca vandito pūjito ca āsi.
    The Buddha was adored and honoured by monks and lay devotees.
    佛陀被出家众和在家信徒喜爱和尊敬。

Exercise b:
1.The rice cooked by the cook was eaten by the servants.
厨师做的饭被服务员吃了。
Sūdena pacito odano sevakehi bhutto.

2.We saw fallen tree.
我们看到倒下的树。
Mayaṃ patitaṃ rukkhaṃ passimhā.

3.Where is the man that has came here?
来这里的那个男人在哪？
Idāni āgato puriso kuhiṃ hoti?

4.The boy ran from here and/fell down there.
男孩从这里跑过来并摔倒在那边。
Dārako ito dhāvitvā tatra patito.

5.Kuto so āgato?
Where has he come from?
他从哪里来？

  1. I saw a deer that had been bitten by a snake and had died.
    我看到一只鹿被蛇咬了然后死了。
    Ahaṃ ahinā daṭṭhaṃ mataṃ migaṃ passiṃ.

7.The man seeing (having seen) his son returned from the village became happy
(glad).
那个男子看到他的儿子从村子回来变得高兴。
Puriso gāmamhā (gāmato) gehamāgataṃ puttaṃ disvā tuṭṭho ahosi.

8.The house built by the carpenter was bought by the house-
holder.
木匠建造的房子被户主买走了。
Kārunā kato geho gahapatinā kīto.

9.The trees cut by the servants fell down.
被仆人砍伐的树木倒下了。
Taravo (rukkhā) sevakehi chinnā patitā.

10.The men who went to the monastery saw the Thera and bowed down.
去到寺院的男子们看到了长老并顶礼。
Vihāraṃ gatā purisā Theraṃ disvā vandiṃsu (abhivādesisuṃ).

11.Where have those men come from?
这些男子从哪里来？
Kuto te manussā āgatā？
