Question 问题

1.How can one attain the 1st jhana by earth kasina?
一个人如何通过地遍获得初禅？

Answer 答案

A relative short answer 一个相对较短的回答

Attaining the first jhana through the earth kasina meditation involves focusing on the earth kasina object to develop concentration and deep absorption. Here are the steps to practice:
通过地遍禅修获得初禅需要专注于地遍所缘，以培养定力和深度安定。 以下是练习步骤：

1.Prepare a small round object with an earthy color, like a clay disk or a stone.
1.准备一个土色的小圆形物体，例如粘土盘或石头。

2.Find a quiet and comfortable place to sit.
2.找一个安静舒适的地方坐下。

3.Place the earth kasina object at eye level, about an arm's length away.
3.将地遍物体置于与视线齐平，距离约一臂的距离。

4.Relax your body and mind, taking a few deep breaths to settle.
4.放松身心，做几次深呼吸让自己平静下来。

5.Fix your gaze on the kasina object, observing its color, texture, and shape.
5.凝视遍遍物体，观察其颜色、质地和形状。

6.As you concentrate on the object, let go of any distracting thoughts or sensations.
6.当你专注于目标时，放弃任何分散注意力的想法或感觉。

7.Maintain your focus on the kasina object, allowing it to fill your awareness.
7. 保持对遍所缘的专注，让它充满你的觉知。

8.As your concentration deepens, you may experience a sense of absorption and tranquility.
8.随着你的注意力加深，你可能会体验到一种全神贯注和宁静的感觉。

9.Continue to sustain your attention on the kasina object, deepening your concentration.
9.继续将注意力维持在遍所缘上，加深定力。

10.With sustained practice, you may enter the first jhana, characterized by joy, tranquility, and one-pointedness of mind.
10.通过持续的修行，你可以进入初禅，其特点是喜悦、平静和专一心。

A relatively long answer 一个相对较长的回答

To attain the first jhana through the use of the earth kasiṇa, one must follow a systematic approach that involves concentration, mindfulness, and mental stability. The earth kasiṇa is a method of meditation that utilizes a carefully prepared and polished disk of earth as an object of focus. The process can be broken down into several steps:

1.Preparation: Begin by finding a quiet and comfortable place to meditate, free from distractions. Ensure that the earth kasiṇa disk is clean, smooth, and free from any imperfections.

2.Establish mindfulness: Sit in a comfortable posture with a straight back, and bring your attention to your breath. Observe the natural rhythm of your breath, inhaling and exhaling mindfully. This will help to calm the mind and establish a sense of focus.

3.Focus on the earth kasiṇa: Once your mind is calm and focused, shift your attention to the earth kasiṇa disk. Contemplate the disk's color, texture, and shape. Maintain a steady gaze without blinking, as this will help to develop concentration.

4.Develop concentration: As you continue to focus on the earth kasiṇa, you will begin to experience increasing levels of concentration. This will be accompanied by a deep sense of calm and tranquility. It is essential to maintain mindfulness throughout this process, as this will help to prevent the mind from becoming distracted or restless.

5.Enter the first jhana: As your concentration deepens, you will eventually enter the first jhana. This is characterized by five factors: initial application of thought, sustained thought, rapture, pleasure, and one-pointedness of mind. You will experience a sense of joy and bliss, as well as a strong sense of mental stability and focus.

6.Maintain and develop the jhana: Once you have entered the first jhana, continue to maintain your focus on the earth kasiṇa, allowing the qualities of the jhana to deepen. With practice, you can develop the ability to access the first jhana at will, ultimately leading to greater mental clarity, inner peace, and insight into the nature of reality.

In summary, attaining the first jhana through the earth kasiṇa involves developing concentration, mindfulness, and mental stability. By following these steps and maintaining a dedicated practice, one can experience the profound benefits of the first jhana and deepen their understanding of the Buddha's teachings. （297 words）

要通过地遍达到初禅，必须遵循系统的方法，包括专注、正念和心理稳定。 地遍是一种禅修方法，利用精心准备和抛光的地盘作为焦点对象。 该过程可以分为几个步骤：

1.准备：首先找一个安静、舒适、不受干扰的地方进行禅修。 确保地遍干净、光滑、无任何瑕疵。

2.建立正念：以舒适的姿势坐着，挺直背部，将注意力集中在呼吸上。 观察呼吸的自然节奏，用心吸气和呼气。 这将有助于平静心灵并建立专注感。

3.专注于地遍：一旦你的心平静且专注，将注意力转移到地遍盘上。 考虑圆盘的颜色、纹理和形状。 保持目光稳定，不要眨眼，因为这有助于集中注意力。

4.培养定力：当你继续专注于地遍时，你将开始体验到定力水平的提高。 这将伴随着深深的平静和安宁。 在整个过程中保持正念至关重要，因为这将有助于防止心分心或焦躁。

5.进入初禅：随着定力的加深，你最终会进入初禅。 其特征有五种：寻、伺、喜、乐、一境性。 您将体验到一种喜悦和幸福感，以及强烈的精神稳定和专注感。

6.维持和发展禅那：进入初禅后，继续专注于地遍，让禅那的功德加深。 通过练习，你可以培养随心所欲进入初禅的能力，最终带来更大的头脑清晰度、内心平静和对现实本质的洞察力。

总而言之，通过地遍获得初禅需要培养定力、正念和心的稳定。 通过遵循这些步骤并保持专注的修行，人们可以体验初禅的深奥利益并加深对佛陀教义的理解。
