Indeclinables 无屈哲变化词

Indeclinables: (of a noun, pronoun, or adjective in a highly inflected language) having no inflections.
(在一个高度屈折变化的语言中，名词、代词或形容词）无屈哲变化

inflection: a change in the form of a word, especially the ending, according to its grammatical function in a sentence（尤指词尾的）屈折变化

Exercises 练习

(a) Translate into English: 翻译成英文（译者注：巴译英）

1.Ahaṃ hiyyo bhātikassa gehe vasitvā ajja pāto’va idhāgacchiṃ.
1.I, having stayed in my brother’s house yesterday, came here early morning today.
1.我昨天住在哥哥家，今天一早就来到了这里。

Pāḷi English 中文
Ahaṃ me 我
hiyyo yesterday 昨天
bhātikassa brother 兄弟
gehe house; dwelling 房子；住处
vasitvā having lived; having stayed; having remained 住在；留在
ajja today, now 今天；现在
pāto’va early morning 清早
idhāgacchiṃ ? ?

2.Idāni thero vihārasmiṃ dhammaṃ deseti, kiṃ tumhe dhammaṃ sotuṃ tahiṃ na gacchatha?
2.The Elder (monk) expounds the Dhamma now in the monastery. Don’t you go there to listen to the Dhamma?
2.长老（和尚）现于寺院说法。 你不去那里听法吗？
dhammaṃ

Pāḷi English 中文
Idāni now; soon; at present 现在
thero (vinaya) elder; senior monk; monk with ten or more years as a monk 长老
vihārasmiṃ monastery 寺院
dhammaṃ Buddha's teachings 佛法
deseti preach (to); teaches (to); explains (to) 教导, 解释
kiṃ ？ ？
tumhe you all; you (royal plural) 你们
dhammaṃ Buddha's teachings 佛法
sotuṃ to hear 听
tahiṃ there; in that place 那里，在那个地方

3.Girisu tatra tatra ahāyo vicaranti.
3. Snakes move about on mountains here and there.
3.蛇在山上到处乱跑。

Pāḷi English 中文
Girisu mountain 高山，山丘
tatra tatra here and there 这里和那里
ahāyo ? ?
vicaranti moves about (in); wanders about (in); roams (in) 四处走动（在）

4.Idāni devo vassati, mā bahi gacchatha.
4.Now rain falls (now it rains), (so) don’t go out.
4.现在下雨了，所以不要出去了。

Pāḷi English 中文
Idāni now, here, at present 现在
devo deity, god 天
vassati rains 下雨
mā do not; may one not; don’t let 不要
bahi externally; outside; outwardly 外面
gacchatha goes; walks; moves; wanders around 去

5.Ajja bahū manussā gāme sannipatiṃsu.
5.Today many men were assembled in the village.
5.今天村里聚集了许多人。

Pāḷi English 中文
Ajja today 今天
bahū many 许多
manussā human being; man; person 人
gāme village 村庄
sannipatiṃsu gathered together 聚集在一起

6.Maggesu rathā calanti.
6.Chariots (cars) move in roads.
6.马车(车)在道路上行驶。

Pāḷi English 中文
Maggesu road; path; track; way 道路
rathā chariot; coach; carriage 马车；车
calanti shakes; wavers; is agitated; is unstable 摇动

7.Viduno Buddhe pasannā.
7.The wise are delighted in the Buddha (pleased with the Buddha).
7.智者对佛陀欢喜。

Pāḷi English 中文
Viduno wise man; seer 智者
Buddhe Buddha; Awakened One 佛陀；觉悟者
pasannā pleased (with); full of faith (in); devoted (to); 对...欢喜

8.Bahū manussā dhamme pasannā dhammaṃ caritvā saggesu uppannā ahesuṃ.
8.Many men delighted in the Dhamma, practiced virtues and were born in heavenly abodes.
许多人对佛法欢喜，修习美德并在天界投生。

Pāḷi English 中文
Bahū many 许多
manussā human being; man; person 人
dhamme the teachings of the Buddha 佛法
pasannā pleased (with); full of faith (in); devoted (to) 对...欢喜
dhammaṃ the teachings of the Buddha 佛法
caritvā having walked; having wandered
saggesu heaven; paradise 天界
uppannā born (in) 出生（在）
ahesuṃ there were; they were

9.Sissā ārāme sannipatitvā kandukehi kīḷiṃsu.
9.Students (pupils) got together in the park and played with balls.
9.学生聚在公园里玩球。

Pāḷi English 中文
Sissā pupil; student 学生
ārāme park; parkland; nature reserve 公园
sannipatitvā having gathered together; having assembled; having met; 聚在一起
kandukehi ball (used in games) ball (used in games) 球（用于游戏中）
kīḷiṃsu played; had fun 玩

10.Gahapatino kumārā vijjālayamhā āgantvā bhutvā idāni mañcesu sayanti.
10.The children of the householder returned from the school, took meal and now are lying on beds.
10.户主的孩子们从学校回来，吃过饭，现在躺在床上。

Pāḷi English 中文
Gahapatino householder 屋主
kumārā youngster; young boy 少年，小男孩
vijjālayamhā
āgantvā having arrived; having come to; having returned 已到达；已来到；已回来
bhutvā having eaten 已吃过
idāni now; soon; at present 现在
mañcesu bed 床
sayanti lies down; rests; sleeps 躺下；休息；睡
